## What's in the Box

Your RKPi5 package includes:

- Raspberry Pi 5 with pre-configured microSD card
- 26GB+ Biblical resources pre-loaded
- Portable power bank (6+ hour battery)
- WiFi antenna
- Quick start card

## First Boot

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
Make sure your device is fully charged before first use.
</Callout>

1. **Power On**: Press and hold the power button for 3 seconds
2. **Wait for Boot**: The LED will flash green while booting (~30 seconds)
3. **Connect**: Look for "RKPi5-XXXX" in your WiFi networks
4. **Access**: Open your browser to `http://rkpi5.local`

## Default Credentials

```bash
WiFi Network: RKPi5-XXXX
Password: (Provided on your quick start card)
Admin Portal: http://rkpi5.local/admin
```
