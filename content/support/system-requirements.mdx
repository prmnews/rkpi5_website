# System Requirements

Technical specifications and compatibility information for RKPi5.

## Device Specifications

### Hardware

- **Processor**: Raspberry Pi 5 (Quad-core ARM Cortex-A76)
- **RAM**: 8GB LPDDR4X (16GB will provide more simulaneous connections)
- **Storage**: 128GB microSD (includes 26GB+ Rapture Kit 3.1 content pre-loaded)
- **Battery**: 20,000mAh or higher portable power bank (note: there are TSA limits for how big a battery pack can be while flying)
- **Estimated Battery Life**: 6+ hours typical use
- **WiFi**: 802.11ac, dual-band (2.4GHz / 5GHz)
- **Concurrent Users**: Up to 15 simultaneous connections
- **Range**: ~100 feet (30 meters) open space

### Pre-loaded Content

<Callout type="success" title="Included Resources">
26GB+ of biblical resources ready to use out of the box.
</Callout>

- **Audio**: 130+ hours of biblical audio content
- **Video**: 39+ hours of teaching videos
- **Text**: Complete biblical library and study resources
- **Tools**: Bible study applications and reference materials

## Client Device Requirements

### Minimum Requirements

To access RKPi5 resources, your device needs:

- **WiFi**: 802.11b/g/n/ac compatible
- **Browser**: Modern web browser (see supported browsers below)
- **Screen**: Any size (optimized for mobile and desktop)

### Supported Browsers

- **Chrome**: 90+
- **Firefox**: 88+
- **Safari**: 14+
- **Edge**: 90+

### Supported Devices

<Callout type="info">
RKPi5 works with virtually any WiFi-enabled device.
</Callout>

**Fully Supported:**
- iPhone/iPad (iOS 14+)
- Android phones/tablets (Android 8+)
- Windows laptops/desktops (Windows 10+)
- macOS devices (macOS 11+)
- Chromebooks
- Linux devices

## Network Requirements

### RKPi5 as Access Point

When operating as a standalone access point:

- **No internet required**: Complete offline operation
- **WiFi only**: No cellular data needed
- **Range**: ~100 feet typical (obstacles reduce range)
- **Users**: Up to 15 concurrent connections

### Optional Internet Connection

<Callout type="warning" title="Optional Feature">
Internet connection only needed for content updates.
</Callout>

If connecting RKPi5 to the internet for updates:

- **Bandwidth**: Any speed (updates are optional)
- **Connection**: WiFi or Ethernet
- **Frequency**: Manual updates as desired

## Environmental Requirements

### Operating Conditions

- **Temperature**: 32°F to 95°F (0°C to 35°C)
- **Humidity**: 20% to 80% non-condensing
- **Altitude**: Up to 10,000 feet

### Storage Conditions

When not in use:

- **Temperature**: 14°F to 113°F (-10°C to 45°C)
- **Charge**: Store at 50-60% battery capacity
- **Location**: Cool, dry place

## Power Requirements

### Battery Operation

- **Capacity**: 20,000mAh
- **Runtime**: 6+ hours typical use
- **Standby**: 48+ hours
- **Charging Time**: 4-6 hours (using included charger)

### AC Operation

Can be operated while charging:

- **Input**: 100-240V AC, 50/60Hz
- **Output**: 5V/3A USB-C
- **Power Consumption**: ~15W typical
