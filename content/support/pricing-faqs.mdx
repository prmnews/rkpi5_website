**Field ($499):**

## Warranty & Returns

### What's the warranty?

**Standard Warranty (All Tiers):**
- 1 year hardware defects
- 1 year battery (excluding normal degradation)
- Lifetime software updates

**Extended Warranty (Optional):**
- 2 years: +$29
- 3 years: +$49
- Includes battery replacement if below 70% capacity

### What does warranty cover?

**Covered:**
- Manufacturing defects
- Component failures
- Battery defects (not degradation)
- Software issues

**Not Covered:**
- Physical damage (drops, water)
- Normal wear and tear
- Battery degradation under 80% (year 1)
- Unauthorized modifications
- Hobbyist tier (DIY builds)

### How do I make a warranty claim?

1. **Contact Support** with order number and issue description
2. **Troubleshooting** - we'll help diagnose the issue
3. **Approval** - if covered, we approve the claim
4. **Return** - ship device back (prepaid label provided)
5. **Repair/Replace** - typically within 2 weeks
6. **Return** - receive repaired or replacement device

### What's your return policy?

**30-Day Money-Back Guarantee:**

- Return within 30 days of delivery
- Device must be in good condition
- Original packaging preferred
- Full refund minus return shipping
- Restocking fee may apply (15% for non-defective opened units)
