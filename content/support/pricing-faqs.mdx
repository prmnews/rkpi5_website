## Pricing Tiers

### What's the difference between each tier?

**Hobbyist (FREE):**
- DIY scripts and documentation
- Build your own device
- Build your own microSD **golden master** boot drive
- Community support only
- For tech-savvy users

**Bare Bones:**
- Pre-configured microSD cards that are ready to be inserted into a new or existing Raspberry Pi board
- Easiest to implement if you have an existing Pi
- Insert the microSD card, boot, and you're ready in < 5 minutes!
- Email support (48hr response)

**Solo or Field:**
- If you desire a pre-made RKPi5 including the Pi5, microSD cards, and power bank
- Contact us to discuss a private arrangement
- The RKPi5 is not available to purchase, it is free

[View detailed estimates comparison](/pricing)

### Can I upgrade from one tier to another?

<Callout type="success" title="Upgrade Scope">
Every tier runs the same RKPi5 software.
</Callout>

**Configuration Upgrades:**

- The difference in tiers is handled by purchasing additonal optional components
- Purchase a battery pack to run the RKPi5 in a backpack or on the run
- Purchase a solar panel system to keep the battery pack topped off
- Purchase a touch screen display panel and connect it to the Pi
- Purchase speakers to make the video or audio available for others to see or hear
